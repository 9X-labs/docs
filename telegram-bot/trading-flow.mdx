Trading on the bot is a four-step flow. Each step is a single tap or message.

## The flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the token">
    Send `/search`, paste a Solana address, or type a symbol (with or without `$`). The bot replies with a **token card**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the token card">
    The card shows: price, market cap, liquidity, holders, security badges, and recent price action. Buttons below: Buy presets, Sell presets, Settings, Chart, Refresh.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick an amount">
    Tap a Buy preset (`0.1`, `0.5`, `1` SOL by default — configurable in `/settings`) or **Custom** to type an amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    The bot edits the message to show your trade summary and an **Authorize** button. Tap to sign and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status update">
    The same message updates again with the result: confirmed (with TX link), failed (with reason), or pending. Typically arrives in under 2 seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Selling

Same flow, but tap a **Sell** preset instead of Buy. Presets are denominated in **% of holdings** (`25 / 50 / 100%` by default).

## Custom amounts

Tap **Custom** and type the amount as a reply. Ranges and limits depend on the token (the bot will reject obviously invalid amounts before authorizing).

## Where settings come from

The slippage, MEV protection, Jito tip tier, and priority fee for the trade come from your `/settings` defaults. To override per-trade, tap **Settings** on the token card before authorizing — temporary overrides apply only to that trade.

For the full settings model, see [Per-token settings](/telegram-bot/per-token-settings).

## Confirmations & idempotency

Each Authorize button is single-use. Once tapped, the bot edits the message to remove the button — you can't accidentally double-submit. If the network rejects the trade, the bot tells you and offers a Retry button (which generates a fresh authorization).

## What if the trade fails?

The bot shows a human-readable reason. Common ones:

- **Slippage exceeded** — increase slippage in settings or retry.
- **Insufficient SOL for fees** — top up your wallet.
- **Token blocked** — flagged honeypot or known scam contract.

See [Order execution](/concepts/order-execution) for the underlying mechanics.

## After the trade

- Send `/positions` to see your updated open positions.
- The trade also flows into **Portfolio → History** on the web.
- If your wallet has [broadcasting on](/concepts/social-trading-loop), the trade posts to the Traders Hub feed.
