Send `/referral` to open the referral dashboard inside the bot. The same data and earning logic as the [web Referral page](/web-app/rewards/referral) — just packaged for Telegram.

## What you see

The bot shows:

- **Your referral code** (editable).
- **Your shareable link** (one-tap to copy or forward inside Telegram).
- **Stats:** number of referrals, your volume, their volume, earnings (pending and lifetime).
- **Leaderboard** position — where you rank among referrers.
- **Team stats** — aggregate metrics for the network you've referred.

## Sharing in Telegram

The big advantage of `/referral` over the web is that the bot can **forward** the referral message inline. Tap **Share Link** → pick a chat → done. The shared message is rich (with the referral chart preview), not a bare URL.

Sharing inside groups posts an invite card with a one-tap join button.

## Earning model

Same as web: **50%** of every fee paid by people you refer, forever. If your referral Quick-Apes one of your broadcasts, they trigger an additional 25% Signal reward — total **50% combined** on that specific trade.

For the full math, see [Social-trading loop](/concepts/social-trading-loop) and [Rewards overview](/web-app/rewards/overview).

## Editing your code

Tap **Edit Code** in the bot's referral menu. Type a new code as a reply. Codes must be alphanumeric, 4–12 characters, and unique across the platform.

## Pagination

The leaderboard view paginates — tap **Next** to scroll. Your rank highlights wherever you sit.

## Where earnings settle

Referral earnings settle to your **primary wallet** at a regular cadence. No manual claim required. Send `/wallet` after a settlement to see them.
