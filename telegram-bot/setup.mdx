The bot has the simplest onboarding of any Znox surface. Three taps, you're trading.

## First-time setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the bot">
    Tap [@znox_trading_betabot](https://t.me/znox_trading_betabot) — or search for `znox_trading_betabot` in Telegram. Hit **Start**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send /start">
    The bot sends a welcome message. Tap **Continue** in the inline keyboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wallet auto-created">
    A Solana wallet is created on the spot. The bot replies with your wallet address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're at the main menu">
    From here you can `/wallet`, `/portfolio`, `/search`, `/positions`, `/settings`, `/referral`, `/help`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If you already have a Znox account on the web

Same flow as above, but the bot recognizes your Telegram identity and links the bot to your existing account on the first `/start`. Your existing wallets show up immediately — no new wallet is created.

## If you signed up via the bot first

Visit [znox.io](https://znox.io) and sign in with the same Telegram account. Your wallets and trade history follow.

## Funding

Send `/wallet` → tap **Deposit** → choose SOL or USDC → the bot returns your address. Send funds from any Solana-compatible source.

See [Wallet operations](/telegram-bot/wallet-operations) for the full deposit/withdraw flow.

## Language

Send `/settings` → **Language** → pick **English** or **Arabic**. The change is immediate; subsequent messages render in your chosen language.

## What if you accidentally close the bot mid-setup?

Send `/start` again. The bot recognizes you mid-onboarding and continues from where you left off (cached for 24 hours). No duplicate wallets get created.
