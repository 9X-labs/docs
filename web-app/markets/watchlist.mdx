The **Watchlist** is your personal shortlist of tokens. It's accessible from the star icon in the top header (with a count badge) and persists across sessions.

## Adding to the watchlist

Star any token from:

- A Markets list (Trending, Explore, Launchpad, Top Traders, Social) — star icon next to the row.
- The [Terminal](/web-app/terminal/overview) header — star button next to the token symbol.

The star fills in to indicate the token is on your list. Click again to remove.

## What the watchlist shows

The same column set as Trending (price, change, liquidity, volume, holders, etc.) but filtered to your starred tokens only.

## What it doesn't do

The watchlist is a saved view, not an alerting system. It doesn't push you notifications when a starred token moves — for that, use price alerts (in the chat icon's notification center) or [Track Wallets](/web-app/traders-hub/track-wallets) for the wallets trading those tokens.
