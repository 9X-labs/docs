The **Social** sub-tab surfaces tokens being talked about right now on X (Twitter) and other social channels. It's the same data signal you'd get scrolling crypto Twitter — distilled into a screener.

## What gets ranked

For each token:

- **Mentions** — count of relevant social posts in the timeframe.
- **Sentiment** — aggregate direction (bullish / neutral / bearish) of those posts.
- **Velocity** — rate of change in mentions (e.g., spiking vs. flat).
- **Notable accounts** — well-known voices that mentioned it.

## Why social signal matters

On Solana memecoins specifically, attention drives price more than fundamentals. A token with sudden mention-velocity often pumps before any volume signal shows up in [Trending](/web-app/markets/trending) — but it can also be coordinated noise.

<Warning>
  Social signal is asymmetric: easy to manufacture, easy to fake. Cross-check against on-chain metrics (holders, liquidity, top-10) before trading.
</Warning>
