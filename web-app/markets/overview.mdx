## Watchlist

The star icon in the header (with a count badge) opens your **[Watchlist](/web-app/markets/watchlist)** — tokens you've personally pinned for quick access. Star any token from any of the Markets tabs or from the Terminal to add it.

## Common controls across tabs

- **Search** (top of page or `/` keyboard shortcut) — symbol or contract address.
- **Auto-refresh toggle** — most lists tick every ~10 seconds. The countdown shows how long until the next pull.
- **View mode** — grid vs list, where supported.

## Clicking a token

Anywhere you see a token row, clicking it opens the **[Terminal](/web-app/terminal/overview)** for that token — the full per-token trade page.
