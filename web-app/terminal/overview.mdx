You land in the **Terminal** by clicking any token anywhere — a Markets row, a search result, a broadcast, a Track Wallets event. The Terminal is where you actually trade.

It's a dense page on purpose. Everything you need to make a decision and act is on one screen. Here's the map.

## Token header

The strip at the top:

- **Logo, symbol, name, verified badge**
- **Current price** (updates live)
- **24h change %** (red/green)
- **Market cap, liquidity, 24h volume, holder count**
- **Watchlist star** — toggle to pin
- **Refresh button** + **chart timeframe selector** (1m / 5m / 15m / 1h / 4h / 1d)

## Chart

Interactive candlestick or line chart, pannable and zoomable. Volume bars below the price chart. Crosshair gives you exact OHLCV values at any point.

## Order entry panel (right side)

The whole [Order entry](/web-app/terminal/order-entry) panel — Buy / Sell tabs, slippage, MEV, Jito, presets, market vs limit. This is the workhorse of the page.

## Your position

If you hold this token, a position card shows:

- **Amount held**
- **Average entry price** (cost basis)
- **Current value** in your quote token
- **Unrealized P&L** ($ and %)
- **Realized P&L** (from already-closed parts of the position)

## Recent trades

Live feed of every swap on this token, network-wide. New buys flash green, sells flash red.

## Per-token Top Traders

Leaderboard of who's making the most P&L on **this specific token**. Click a trader to view their profile or track their wallet.

## First buyers

Wallets that bought in the very first blocks after launch. A pump signal sometimes — a sniper-coordination signal other times.

## KOL / social engagement

Recent X mentions, sentiment, and any influential voices that have posted about the token.

## Security audit

A risk panel summarizing:

- Verified contract ✓/✗
- Renounced ownership ✓/✗
- Liquidity locked ✓/✗
- Mint authority disabled ✓/✗
- Top-holder concentration
- A composite trust score

Glance at this **before** you buy a freshly launched token. The panel is the cheapest insurance against scams.

## Settings (gear icon)

Opens the [Terminal Settings](/web-app/terminal/settings) modal — defaults for slippage, priority fee, MEV, Jito tier, expert mode.
