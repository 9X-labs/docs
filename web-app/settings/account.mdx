The **Account** sub-tab is where your identity on Znox lives. Username, profile visibility, and which third-party providers (Google, Telegram) are linked to your account.

## Username

Your display name across the platform — appears on broadcasts, comments, the Traders Hub leaderboard. Click the field to edit and **Save Changes**.

A preview card shows how your username will look on broadcast cards.

## Profile visibility

| Setting | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Visible Profile** | You appear in the Traders Hub leaderboard. Other users can find your profile and follow you. |
| **Hidden Profile** | You don't appear in any leaderboard. Your trades still broadcast (if enabled), but anonymously. |

Default is Visible. Switch to Hidden if you want to participate in social trading without surfacing your identity.

## Avatar

Upload a profile picture. Square images work best (cropped automatically). Shows on broadcasts, comments, Traders Hub.

## Connected accounts

Google and Telegram OAuth links. For each:

- **Linked** — sign-in works via this provider.
- **Connect** — link a provider you haven't used yet.
- **Unlink** — remove the link.

<Warning>
  Unlinking your **only** sign-in provider will lock you out. Always have at least one provider linked.
</Warning>

## Email & phone

Read-only display of the email or phone tied to your account (from your sign-in provider). To change these, change them at the source provider (Google account, Telegram).

## Telegram username

If you've linked Telegram, your TG username is shown here. Updates from Telegram propagate automatically.

## Save Changes

Hit the orange **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the page after editing any field. Without saving, edits revert on navigation.
