The **PnL Calendar** is a calendar-grid heatmap of your trading P&L. Each cell is a day; the color intensity shows magnitude; green means profit, red means loss.

## Opening it

Click the **PNL Calendar** button at the top of [Portfolio](/web-app/portfolio/overview). The view opens in a modal.

## Reading the grid

- **Green** — profitable day. Darker green = bigger profit.
- **Red** — losing day. Darker red = bigger loss.
- **Grey** — no trades that day (or net zero).

Hover any cell to see the exact P&L for that day plus the trade count.

## Toggles

- **Wallet filter** — show all wallets aggregated, or pick a specific wallet.
- **Quote token toggle** — view P&L in your selected quote (SOL vs. USDC). The calendar is currency-aware.
- **Period** — month / year view.
