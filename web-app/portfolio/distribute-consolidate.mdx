Distribute and Consolidate are the two operations that let you reshape your money across the [multi-wallet](/concepts/multi-wallet) model. Both are real on-chain transfers.

## Distribute

Split funds from one source wallet across many destination wallets.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Distribute">
    From [Portfolio](/web-app/portfolio/overview), click **Distribute**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the source">
    Which of your wallets the funds come from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick destinations">
    Select target wallets. You can include any combination of your existing wallets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set splits">
    Either equal-split, or set a custom % per destination. The total must sum to 100% of the amount being moved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Znox builds and submits the transactions. Each destination receives its share on-chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### When to distribute

- After a big deposit, to spread capital across strategy-specific wallets.
- Before a wave of risky trades, so a single wallet's loss doesn't expose your full balance.
- To set up sub-wallets for [broadcasting](/concepts/social-trading-loop) different strategies separately.

## Consolidate

Pull funds from many wallets into one (typically your primary).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Consolidate">
    From Portfolio, click **Consolidate**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the destination">
    Usually the primary wallet, but you can choose any.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick sources">
    Select which wallets to drain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Each source signs an outgoing transfer to the destination.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### When to consolidate

- Before a large withdrawal — easier to clear the security gate from one wallet than many.
- When closing out a strategy — collapse the dedicated wallet into your main one.
- For tax reporting — sometimes simpler to track P&L from a single consolidated wallet.

## Both operations are on-chain

These are real Solana transfers — they cost network fees and create transaction history visible to anyone (including [tracked wallets](/web-app/traders-hub/track-wallets) following your address). They're not internal accounting moves.
