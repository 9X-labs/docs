The **Portfolio** tab is your money home. It lists every wallet on your account, tracks P&L per wallet, and gives you the four core fund operations: Deposit, Withdraw, Distribute, Consolidate.

For why Znox uses a multi-wallet model, see [Multi-wallet](/concepts/multi-wallet).

## Layout

The page has two panes:

- **Left** — list of all your wallets with per-wallet stats.
- **Right** — detail view of the currently selected wallet, with [Holdings / History / Orders](/web-app/portfolio/holdings-history-orders) tabs.

## Wallet list columns

| Column | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Wallet | Nickname + truncated public key (with a copy button) |
| Invested | Total quote-token value put in |
| PnL | Realized + unrealized profit/loss |
| W/L | Wins / losses count (closed positions only) |
| SOL | Current SOL balance |

## Right-side stats card

For the selected wallet:

- **Total balance** — SOL amount + USD equivalent.
- **Total PnL USD** with absolute and percentage.
- **Unrealized profits** — open positions only.
- **Total volume** — lifetime trade volume.

## Adding wallets

The **Add Wallet** button (top of the list) creates a new Solana wallet on your account. The new wallet starts empty — fund it via Deposit or Distribute.

## PnL Calendar

The **PnL Calendar** button opens a [heatmap view](/web-app/portfolio/pnl-calendar) of your daily P&L history.

## Privacy

The eye-icon at the top of the right pane toggles balance visibility — useful when you're sharing your screen.

## Mobile layout

On mobile, the wallet list is collapsible. Tap a wallet to expand into the detail view; tap the back arrow to return to the list.
