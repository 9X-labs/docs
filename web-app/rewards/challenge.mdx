**Challenges** are time-bound trading competitions. You enter, trade as you normally would, and at the end of the window the top finishers split a prize pool.

## Active and past challenges

The page lists:

- **Active challenges** — currently running or in their entry window. Tap **Join** to participate.
- **Past challenges** — historical results, top finishers, and prize distribution.

## Joining a challenge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the challenge details">
    Tap a challenge card. The detail modal shows entry rules, duration, prize pool, and current leaderboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose risk / stake">
    Some challenges require a stake or have risk-band rules. Pick the level that suits you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm entry">
    You're in. Trades placed during the challenge window automatically count toward your standing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How standings are computed

Different challenges score differently. Common metrics:

- **Total P&L** — absolute profit during the window.
- **P&L %** — return on starting balance.
- **Win rate** — share of profitable trades.
- **Volume** — total traded.

Read the rules per challenge. The leaderboard updates in real-time as participants trade.

## Prize distribution

Distributions are listed in each challenge's rules. Typical patterns:

- Top 1: large share (e.g., 40%).
- Top 2–3: meaningful chunks (e.g., 20% / 10%).
- Top 4–10: smaller distributions.
- Sometimes a "consolation" pool for participants outside the top tier.

Payouts settle to your primary wallet at challenge end.

## Fair play

Challenges have anti-collusion safeguards (e.g., wash-trade detection, multi-account flagging). Don't be the person who tries to game them — flagged participants forfeit prizes.
