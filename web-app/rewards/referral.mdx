Every Znox user gets a referral code on signup. Anyone who joins via your code (or link) is permanently attached to you — and **50% of every fee they pay flows to your wallet**, for as long as they trade.

## Your referral code & link

The Rewards → Referral page shows:

- **Your code** — a short alphanumeric string. Editable in-place if you want a vanity code.
- **Your referral link** — the full URL you share. Copy or share with one click.

## Sharing

Anywhere you'd put a link — Twitter bio, group chat, blog post — works. When someone clicks it and signs up, they're attributed to you automatically.

<Tip>
  Customize your code to something memorable (`KAR22FTQ` → `KARMA`). Easier to remember when you tell someone in voice or in person.
</Tip>

## The earning chart

The big chart on the page plots **rewards earned vs. network volume** with three lines:

- **Orange (Referral, 50%)** — what you earn from a pure referral, no signal share.
- **Purple (Signal, 25%)** — what a broadcaster earns from a non-referred aper.
- **Green dashed (Ref + Signal, 25% + 25%)** — both apply: someone you referred Quick-Aped your broadcast.

The x-axis is your network's combined trading volume. Hover any point for the exact dollar earnings at that volume.

## Stats panel

To the right of the chart:

| Stat | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Your volume | Your own trading volume |
| Referral volume | Total volume from people you referred |
| Referrals | Number of people who signed up via your code |
| Reward | Lifetime SOL earned from referrals |

## How payouts work

Earnings accrue in the background as your referrals trade. They settle to your primary wallet on a regular cadence (no manual claim required for referral rewards).
