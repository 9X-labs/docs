## How the splits stack

If someone you referred Quick-Apes a broadcast you posted, both shares apply:

| Scenario | Share you earn |
| --- | --- |
| Referral only — your invitee trades on their own | 50% of their fee |
| Signal only — a stranger Quick-Apes your broadcast | 25% of their fee |
| Both — your invitee Quick-Apes your broadcast | 25% (referral) + 25% (signal) = **50%** combined |

The chart on the [Referral page](/web-app/rewards/referral) plots all three curves so you can see the math at any volume level.

## When you get paid

- **Referral & Signal** — accrue continuously as your referrals/apers trade. View pending and lifetime totals on each respective tab. Payouts go to your primary wallet.
- **Challenge** — paid out at the end of each competition based on final ranking.

## No cap, no expiry

This is important: referral and signal earnings are **forever**. Once someone is your referral, you earn from their trades indefinitely. Once someone has Quick-Aped your broadcast, you earn from that position for as long as they hold it.
