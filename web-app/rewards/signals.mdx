The **Signals** sub-tab tracks earnings from people Quick-Aping your broadcasts. Every wallet whose [broadcasting is enabled](/web-app/portfolio/overview) participates automatically.

For the underlying flow, see [Social-trading loop](/concepts/social-trading-loop).

## Stats at the top

| Card | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Total earnings USD | Lifetime signal rewards across all your broadcasts |
| Pending earnings USD | Accrued but not yet settled |
| Total broadcasts | Count of trades you've broadcast |
| Total apes | Number of times your broadcasts have been Quick-Aped |
| Split % | Confirmation that you receive 25% of fees |

## Signal list

A scrollable list of every broadcast that has earned you something. Each row shows:

- Token symbol + your buy size
- Number of users who aped
- Your earnings from this broadcast so far

Click any row to open a **Broadcast Detail** modal with:

- The original broadcast card
- Token chart with entry marked
- Reactions and comments
- List of users who aped (and how much each ape contributed to your earnings)
- Your running earnings from this signal over time

## When you don't earn signal rewards

- The aper used a Quick Ape link older than the [slink TTL](/concepts/slinks) (1 hour) — attribution drops.
- Your wallet's broadcasting was off when you placed the trade — no broadcast, no signal.
- The aper traded the same token from a different entrypoint (e.g., direct via search), without going through your broadcast.

## When you stop earning on a position

You earn for as long as the aper holds and trades the position they opened from your broadcast. Once they fully close it, that position stops generating signal rewards — but new apes on new broadcasts of yours start a fresh stream.
