The **Leaderboard** sub-tab ranks Znox broadcasters — wallets that have actively broadcast trades. It's the discovery side of the social-trading loop: how you find people whose calls are worth Quick-Aping.

## Columns

| Column | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Rank | Position in the leaderboard |
| Trader | Username + tier badge |
| P&L 30d | Profit/loss across all broadcasts in the last 30 days |
| Win % | Share of broadcasts that closed in profit |
| Followers | Number of users following this broadcaster |
| Volume | Total volume of broadcast trades |

## Sorting

Click any column header to sort by it. Default is P&L 30d descending.

## Trader detail modal

Click any row to open a side panel with:

- Recent broadcast history (with chart for each)
- Follower count and trend
- Per-token P&L breakdown
- Buttons to **Follow**, **Track Wallet**, or **Quick Ape** the most recent broadcast

## Following

Following a trader filters their broadcasts into your **FOLLOWING** view in the [Broadcast feed](/web-app/traders-hub/broadcast). It does not auto-execute their trades — Quick Ape still requires a manual tap.

## How leaderboard differs from Markets → Top Traders

- **Markets → Top Traders** is the *whole Solana network* — global wallet leaderboard from on-chain analytics.
- **Traders Hub → Leaderboard** is just *Znox broadcasters* — wallets actively participating in the social loop.

Use Top Traders to find anyone profitable on-chain. Use this leaderboard to find profitable people whose trades you can directly Quick Ape.
