The **Broadcast** sub-tab is the heart of Znox's social-trading loop. Every trade from a wallet with broadcasting enabled shows up here as a card. You can react, comment, or copy the trade.

For the underlying mechanic, see [Social-trading loop](/concepts/social-trading-loop).

## Anatomy of a broadcast card

- **Trader** — username + avatar + (optional) X badge
- **Action** — `BOUGHT $5 of SOL @ $49.68B mcap` (or `SOLD`)
- **Chart** — token price action since the broadcast, with the entry point marked
- **Reactions** — eight emoji buttons (🔥🚀💰💀👍❤️😂😯) with live counts
- **Comments icon** — opens nested-thread comments
- **Share icon** — copy the broadcast link
- **Quick Ape button** — one-click buy at your preset amount
- **Ape count** — how many people have already aped this trade

## Filter toggle

- **NEWEST** — chronological, all broadcasts you can see.
- **FOLLOWING** — only broadcasts from traders you follow.

## Quick Ape

Tap **Quick Ape** to place the same buy at your preset SOL amount. This:

1. Routes the trade against the same token the broadcast referenced.
2. Credits 25% of the fee to the broadcaster as a [Signal reward](/web-app/rewards/signals).
3. If you were referred by someone, splits another 25% to your referrer.

Your Quick Ape preset is the amount you're comfortable risking on a one-tap copy — set it in [Terminal Settings](/web-app/terminal/settings). Don't make it bigger than you'd lose without flinching.

## Reactions and comments

Reactions are public — your username appears in the count tooltip. Comments are threaded; you can reply to specific comments. Both are real-time — see [Real-time architecture](/concepts/real-time-architecture).

## Telegram group integration

If you've added the Znox bot to a Telegram group as admin, you can wire one of your wallets to broadcast there. Trades from that wallet post both to the Traders Hub feed *and* to the linked TG group with a Quick Ape button. Setup is in **Portfolio → wallet → Broadcast settings**, or see [Telegram groups](/telegram-bot/groups/setup).
