Znox accounts are linked to your Google or Telegram identity. When you sign in for the first time, a Solana wallet is generated for you and held in our key-management system. You don't need a browser extension.

## Sign in

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web App">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open znox.io">
        Click **Sign in** in the top-right.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Pick a provider">
        Choose **Continue with Google** or **Continue with Telegram**. Both options give you the same account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Authorize">
        Approve the OAuth prompt. You'll be redirected back to Znox once.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Done">
        You're in. A primary Solana wallet is already created and visible in **Portfolio**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Telegram Bot">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the bot">
        Tap [@znox_trading_betabot](https://t.me/znox_trading_betabot) on your phone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send /start">
        The bot greets you and creates your wallet on the spot.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Done">
        You'll see the main menu. Your wallet is ready.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Link both surfaces

If you signed up on the web with Google, link Telegram (or vice-versa) so a single account works everywhere.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Account">
    On the web app, click your profile dropdown → **Settings** → **Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect the other provider">
    Click **Connect Telegram** (or **Connect Google**). Approve the prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Both providers now show as **Linked**. Sign-in from either side works.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can unlink later from the same panel. Your wallet and trade history stay with the account, not the provider.
</Note>

## What you get on signup

- **A primary Solana wallet** — generated and ready to receive SOL or USDC.
- **Account-wide settings** — slippage, priority fee, MEV protection (see [Order execution](/concepts/order-execution)).
- **Optional security upgrades** — passkey, 2FA, withdrawal allowlist (see [Security model](/concepts/security-model)).
